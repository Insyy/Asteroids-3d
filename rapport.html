Asteroid 3D is based on the popular 1980s Atari's Asteroid game!

Click the image below to give it a try.

The objective of Asteroids is to destroy asteroids and ennemy saucers. The player controls a ship that can rotate left and right, fire shots straight forward, and thrust forward or backward.
